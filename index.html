About Our Academy

01

Our Story

Damaroosri Natyakshetra is dedicated to preserving and promoting the classical art form of Bharatanatyam. With experienced instructors and a passion for cultural excellence, we nurture the next generation of dancers and cultural ambassadors.

02

Our Mission

To provide world-class classical dance education, promoting cultural awareness and artistic excellence while making traditional Bharatanatyam accessible to all aspiring dancers.

03

Our Vision

To create a community of passionate dancers who are not just performers but cultural ambassadors, preserving and advancing the rich heritage of classical Indian dance.

Beginner Classes

Foundational posture, adavus, expressions, and storytelling for new learners. This program builds the bedrock of classical Bharatanatyam — from proper stances and hand gestures (mudras) to understanding the basic rhythm structures (talam) that define this ancient art.

Age Group 5 Years & Above
Duration 3 Months
Schedule Weekday Batches

Intermediate Training
